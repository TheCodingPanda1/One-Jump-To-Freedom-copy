How to Play

Arrow keys to move, up arrow to jump.

Beware enemies, they are not nice.

The number in the top left corner is how many jumps you have, every time you jump, it will decline. You can only use them on the ground.

The number in the top right corner is how many air jumps you have. They decline every time you jump while in the air. They will only be used on the ground if you have no regular jumps left.
